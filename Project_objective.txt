Our online retail company wants to increase sales, improve customer retention, and optimize marketing strategies by analyzing historical customer transaction data. The company has collected detailed information about customer demographics, product purchases, order details, and payment behavior.

The dataset includes the following columns: Customer ID, Age, Gender, Item Purchased, Category, Purchase Amount, Location, Size, Color, Season, Review Rating, Subscription Status, Shipping Type, Discount Applied, Previous Purchase, and Payment Method.

The business problem is to extract meaningful insights from this dataset to answer questions such as:

Who are the high-value customers, and what are their demographic characteristics (age, gender, location)?
Which products and categories generate the most revenue, and what are the popular size/color preferences?
How do seasonality and discounts impact sales performance?
How do subscription status, payment method, and shipping type influence purchase behavior and revenue?
What is the relationship between customer satisfaction (review ratings) and repeat purchases?
