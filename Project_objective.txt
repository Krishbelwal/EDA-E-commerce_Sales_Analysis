Our online retail company wants to increase sales, improve customer retention, and optimise marketing strategies.

The business problems is to extract meaningful insights from this dataset to answer questions such as:

Who are the high-value customers, and what are their demographic characteristics (age, gender, location)?
Which products and categories generate the most revenue, and what are the popular size/color preferences?
How do seasonality and discounts impact sales performance?
How do subscription status, payment method, and shipping type influence purchase behaviour and revenue?
What is the relationship between customer satisfaction (review ratings) and repeat purchases?
